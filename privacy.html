Privacy Policy



At Regal Tours N Travels, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Website.

1. Information We Collect
We may collect the following types of information:
Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, travel preferences, and payment details (collected securely).
Non-Personal Information: Browser type, IP address, and usage data collected through cookies or analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
Process and manage bookings and payments. Respond to inquiries and provide customer support. Send updates, offers, or promotional content (if you’ve opted in). Improve our Website and services.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data
All personal data is stored securely on servers with reasonable administrative and technical safeguards.
Payment data is processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways; we do not store full card details.
We retain your data only as long as necessary for legal or business purposes.

4. Sharing of Information
We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.
We may share limited data with trusted service providers (e.g., payment processors or travel partners) solely to fulfill your bookings.

5. Cookies
Our Website uses cookies to improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

6. Your Rights
You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at regaltat@gmail.com
You can opt out of marketing emails at any time.

7. Updates to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Effective Date.”
